Hello, I'm Bridget Fagan.

A native Chicagoan, my inherent curiosity about people and cultures led me to major in English and Spanish Literature where I sharpened my skill with in-depth analysis, research, and storytelling. I taught English as a Second Language for many years and worked on the administrative, training, and customer support side of our family business. These positions allowed me to do what I love — help improve people’s day-to-day lives and solve problems.


I am interested not only in the user's experience, but in the philosophy of design and in examining the social implications of product-making. I am passionate about learning people's stories. I enjoy speaking, learning, and helping teach people about different languages. In my free time, you might find me cooking, playing guitar, or enjoying some salsa (the dance, the music, or the food–all equally outstanding).


Strengths: UX process, design thinking, empathetic research

If you want to know more about me and my projects, or simply want to grab a cup of coffee, just send me an email. I would love to hear from you.
