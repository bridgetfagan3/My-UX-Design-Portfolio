ROLE:
I was brought onto the Newsela team to create the research strategy, architecture, and prototype for an update of the Newsela Educator Center.
,
The beginning of my involvement included discovery research, content inventory, competitive analysis, journey mapping, and feature prioritization. I moved into rapid prototyping and completed the first iteration of a low-fidelity prototype using InVision.

I completed usability testing on the prototype and consulted with UI designers and developers to keep the voice of the user at the forefront of our work as the project moved from high-fidelity to development and launch.
