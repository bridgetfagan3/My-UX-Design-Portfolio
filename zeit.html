Zeit: enhancing the travel experience
A responsive e-commerce website that allows customers to browse all different trip categories and details.


responsive design & branding

MY ROLE

User Research | Information Archtecture | Interaction Design | Prototype and Testing | UI/Visual Design

TOOLS

Competitive Analysis | Market Research | User Interviews | Empathy Mapping | Personal Development | Site Map | User Flows | Wireframing | Affinity Mapping | Usability Testing | Style Guide | UI Kit Development | Hi-Fi Prototype

Timeline: 80 hours over 4 weeks

ABOUT

Zeit is a subsidiary of Richard Branson’s Virgin empire. After a long struggle with Elon Musk, Virgin has been able to make time travel tourism available to all. Investors from Zeit, a subsidiary of Virgin Group, sees the potential for growth and development in the time travel ecommerce market.

GOAL

Research the experience of e-commerce travel users to create a responsive design and brand for Zeit.

CHALLENGES

Zeit is a new company that wants to enter the unexplored market for time travel. However, users already have set patterns for how to navigate related travel websites. I integrated and practiced the UX design toolkit I learned through DesignLab’s UX Academy while building every step of this product with guidance from my mentor. Some of the challenges with this project included maintaining usability and Zeit’s brand aesthetics across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Also, avoiding use of trip categories and filter options that are confusing to users.
